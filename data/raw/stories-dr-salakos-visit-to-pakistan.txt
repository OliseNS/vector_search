Dr. Umair Haider, GM of DCC & KCC Consultants office greeted and hosted him during his 5-day stay. Annual Employee Dinner 2022 The Annual Employee Dinner 2022 was a celebration of the teams’ contributions and recognition of their hard work. The event hosted by Sana S. included speeches from Madeeha R. (HR Manager), Noshaba M. (Manager, Marketing and Communications), Dr. Haider (General Manager), and Michele C. (Sr. Director of HR, Marketing/communications). The event ended with a closing address by the CEO, Dr. Babajide Salako, followed by a surprise thank-you message from the Founder and Chairman, Dr. Tunji Alausa.The event also included a recognition ceremony and a raffle with exciting employee prizes. Meeting with DCC and KCC Teams Dr. Salako met with all teams to discuss their progress and upcoming projects. He also took suggestions and discussed their growth at corporate and individual levels. Dr. Salako’s meetings ended with a big thanks to everyone, laying down a very optimistic and robust growth plan for the DCC & KCC Consultants office. He also appreciated the efforts and leadership of Dr. Umair Haider, General Manager, and Noshaba Maqbool, Manager, Marketing and Communications in Pakistan.
