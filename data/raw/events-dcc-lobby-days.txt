About the event The Dialysis Care Center is pleased to invite community members to DCC Lobby Days. This is a great opportunity for individuals seeking to expand their knowledge of dialysis. The events will feature in-depth discussions on various dialysis options, covering the respective advantages and drawbacks of each modality. Attendees will be given the opportunity to explore and identify the most appropriate dialysis method available at the Dialysis Care Center, tailored to meet the specific healthcare needs of patients. We encourage community members to participate in these informative sessions and gain valuable insights into the dialysis treatment process.
