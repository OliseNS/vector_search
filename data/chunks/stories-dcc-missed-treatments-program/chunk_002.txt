Dialysis removes waste and extra fluids from your blood, which helps keep your body healthy. When you miss a treatment, these waste products can build up in your system, leading to serious health problems. These problems can include feeling weak and tired, high blood pressure, heart problems, and even seizures.The Missed Treatment Intervention TeamThe Missed Treatment Intervention Team at DCC is more than just a group of specialists— they’re your partners in dialysis care. Their job is to ensure patients receive the essential dialysis treatments to stay healthy. Imagine them as your own personal support system for dialysis. Every day, they diligently monitor our Electronic Health Records (EHR).
